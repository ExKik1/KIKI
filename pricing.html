❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate any billing differences.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your data stays safe for 30 days after cancellation. You can export everything before your account is deactivated. We never delete your data without notice.
Do you offer student or nonprofit discounts? +
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified students and 70% off for eligible nonprofits. Contact our support team with proof of eligibility.
Is there a free trial for Pro and Enterprise? +
Absolutely. All paid plans come with a full 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll only be billed after the trial ends.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All transactions are secured by Stripe.
