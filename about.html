Our Mission

Empowering creativity at every level

KIKI started with a simple belief: great design tools shouldn't be locked behind steep learning curves or enterprise price tags. Every creator deserves access to professional-grade tools.

We're building the platform we always wished existed — one that combines the power of AI with the simplicity of consumer apps.

💡 What We Believe

Our Core Values

Speed over perfection

We ship fast, learn from users, and iterate relentlessly. Progress beats polish in early stages.

📅 Our Journey

The KIKI Story

2023 — Founded
KIKI is born in a San Francisco apartment
Aria and Marcus build the first prototype over a weekend hackathon. The core idea: AI-native design for everyone.
2024 — Early Access
First 1,000 beta users & $3M seed round
Overwhelming response from the design community. Raised seed funding to grow the team to 8 people.
2025 — Launch
Public launch — 10K users in 48 hours
Product Hunt #1 of the day. Team expands to 18 people. Series A round of $18M closed.
2026 — KIKI v2.0
Complete rebuild with new AI engine
50K+ active users, 120 countries, and the most powerful creative platform on the web. This is just the beginning.
